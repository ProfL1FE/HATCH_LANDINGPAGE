FAQ

Do I need to study business?

No. HATCH welcomes students from every faculty and discipline.

Can I join alone?

Yes. Explorer Pass is designed for individual participants.

Who owns my idea?

You retain your intellectual property unless you voluntarily enter into a separate written agreement.

Can I use AI?

Yes. AI is encouraged when used responsibly. Significant AI tools used in developing your solution should be disclosed.

Is there a cash prize?

The main reward is the 12-Month HATCH Launchpad™, valued at approximately USD30,000 in venture development support. It is not positioned as a cash prize.

Can students from different universities form a team?

Yes. The prototype supports team registration and can later support team matching through JO1NID.
